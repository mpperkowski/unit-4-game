Instructions

Mewtwo strikes back, (we gotta go **** him up... we don't take **** from anybody) and is after Ash Ketchum and his Pokemon.
Each time you click on one of Ash's Pokemon, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
You can defeat Mewtwo by matching your total score to his number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above Mewtwo's number.
The value of each Pokemon is hidden from you until you click on one of them.
Each time this game starts, the values of each Pokemon will change.
